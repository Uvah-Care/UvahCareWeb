About

Shalini Sharma | Yoga Instructor & Therapist

  • City: New Delhi, Delhi
  • Experience: 8+ Years
  • Age: 29
  • Specialization: Fitness & Pregnancy Yoga

I have been practicing Yoga since my childhood. Subsequently, l learnt Yoga Science from Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga and was a scholar in it. l have specialization and certification in Pregnancy Yoga. I have Masters in Physical Education from Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University. l have been teaching Yoga Science for 8+ years now. Being an Innovative and curious person, l started researching the benefits of Therapeutic Yoga and made it my mission to deliver and spread “Total in Totality". I am completely inclined towards nature and would love to create a natural and healthy community.
Through Uvah Care, l wish to create a valuable impact on the lives of people, by devoting and applying all my knowledge and experience; because l strongly believe that the greatest Wealth which one can possess is Health itself.
